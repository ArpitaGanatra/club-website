Culture II

Analysis by Jonathan Morales

Although I didn't listen to the album in it's entirety, it was a dissapointment. After the rap group's recent work Culture I, I learned that the Migos have a very specific and generic style thatthey don't stray away from. Quavo and Offset, two of the group's members, released collaborative projects with other artist's where they were forcedto adopt new styles. Those projects were successful because of the unique stylistic adjustements the artists made to fit their project. This album feels as though they were more comfortable than innovative. This project definitely has a few strong tracks but it's nothing we haven't heard before.
